A British Political Draft Game

Assemble a cabinet from the whole sweep of history. Then fight the country, seat by seat.

— figures 7 eras · 1832–today 650 seats · 12 at the table

You are handed the keys to government and a cast of thousands to staff it with. Spin for a party and an era, draft whoever fortune offers, and fill all twelve seats of the cabinet. Then hold a general election — fought the way Britain really fights them, one constituency at a time. Win a majority and you govern; fall short and you lead the opposition. The dream is the impossible one: every seat in the House.
